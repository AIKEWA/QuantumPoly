Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to digital accessibility for people with disabilities

Last Updated: November 4, 2025 | Next Review: May 4, 2026

WCAG 2.2 Level AA Compliant

Introduction

QuantumPoly is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We believe that accessibility is not an obligation but a fundamental aspect of digital equity. Every person, regardless of ability, deserves equal access to information and functionality.

Compliance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

QuantumPoly is fully compliant with WCAG 2.2 Level AA.

This means that our content conforms to all Level A and Level AA success criteria, ensuring that our platform is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of QuantumPoly relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML5 with semantic elements
  • CSS3 for styling and layout
  • JavaScript (progressively enhanced)
  • ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) attributes
  • WAI-ARIA 1.2 specification

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Evaluation Methods

We employ a comprehensive, multi-layered testing approach to ensure accessibility compliance:

Automated Testing

  • Google Lighthouse: Achieved score of 96/100 for accessibility
  • Axe DevTools: Zero critical violations detected
  • Wave (WebAIM): Regular automated scans for accessibility issues
  • Pa11y CI: Continuous integration testing for WCAG compliance

Manual Testing

  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard-only navigation testing across all pages
  • Screen Reader Testing: Verified with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver
  • Color Contrast Analysis: Manual verification of all text and interactive elements
  • Focus Management: Tested focus order and visibility across all interactive components
  • Responsive Design: Tested across multiple screen sizes and zoom levels (up to 200%)

Expert Review

Our platform has been reviewed by accessibility experts familiar with WCAG 2.2 guidelines and assistive technologies. A comprehensive manual audit was conducted on November 10, 2025, covering all critical governance-facing surfaces and public transparency interfaces.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to ensure accessibility:

Keyboard Navigation

  • Full keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements
  • Logical tab order throughout the site
  • Skip navigation links for main content
  • Keyboard shortcuts documented where applicable

Screen Reader Support

  • Semantic HTML structure
  • ARIA labels and roles for complex components
  • Descriptive alt text for all images
  • Live regions for dynamic content updates

Visual Design

  • Minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text
  • Minimum contrast ratio of 3:1 for large text
  • Clear focus indicators (2px outline)
  • No information conveyed by color alone

Content Structure

  • Proper heading hierarchy (H1-H6)
  • Descriptive page titles
  • Meaningful link text
  • Consistent navigation structure

Forms & Interaction

  • Clear form labels and instructions
  • Error messages associated with fields
  • Sufficient time for form completion
  • Confirmation for critical actions

Media & Motion

  • Respect for prefers-reduced-motion
  • No auto-playing media
  • Captions and transcripts for video (when applicable)
  • Text alternatives for all non-text content

Feedback and Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of QuantumPoly. If you encounter accessibility barriers, please let us know:

Accessibility Contact

Email: accessibility@quantumpoly.ai

Response Time: We aim to respond to all accessibility inquiries within 30 days.

Alternative Contact: If you prefer, you can also reach us at legal@quantumpoly.ai

When reporting an accessibility issue, please include:

  • The URL or page where you encountered the issue
  • A description of the problem
  • The assistive technology you are using (if applicable)
  • Your operating system and browser

Enforcement Procedure

We take accessibility feedback seriously and follow a formal internal governance protocol:

  1. Receipt & Acknowledgment: All accessibility reports are acknowledged within 2 business days
  2. Assessment: Issues are triaged by severity and impact within 5 business days
  3. Resolution Plan: A remediation plan with timeline is provided within 10 business days
  4. Implementation: Critical issues are addressed within 30 days; others according to agreed timeline
  5. Verification: All fixes are tested with assistive technologies before deployment
  6. Follow-up: We contact the reporter to confirm the issue is resolved

All accessibility issues and resolutions are logged in our governance ledger for transparency and accountability.

Our Commitment

Accessibility is an ongoing journey, not a destination. We are committed to:

  • Maintaining WCAG 2.2 Level AA compliance across all public-facing interfaces
  • Regularly testing with assistive technologies and real users
  • Training our development team on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating accessibility into our design and development process from the start

Ethical Principle: At QuantumPoly, we treat accessibility failures not as design flaws but as moral non-compliance. Digital equity is a fundamental right, and we embed this principle into every line of code we write.
