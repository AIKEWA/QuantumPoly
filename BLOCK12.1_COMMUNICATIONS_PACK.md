## FAQ

**Q1. Why do we need a certification program?**
A1. As we move to **Adaptive Governance (Stage VIII)**, the system becomes capable of rewriting its own rules. To prevent "value drift," we need human operators who can audit these changes. Certification ensures that the humans "in the loop" are qualified to understand the system's reasoning, creating a legally defensible "Epistemic Bridge."

**Q2. Who is this training for?**
A2. It serves three groups:

1.  **General Stakeholders** (Path 1): Need to understand the dashboards and regulations.
2.  **Node Administrators** (Path 2): Need to maintain the infrastructure and simulations.
3.  **Governance Practitioners** (Path 3): Need to make high-stakes, long-term ethical decisions.

**Q3. What is "Epistemic Drift"?**
A3. Epistemic Drift occurs when the human operators lose the ability to understand or predict the behavior of the autonomous system. This leads to a loss of meaningful control. Our curriculum is designed specifically to prevent this by keeping human competence aligned with system complexity.

**Q4. Are these certificates recognized externally?**
A4. The certificates are issued as **Verifiable Credentials** on the Federated Transparency Ledger. While currently internal to the QuantumPoly federation, they are designed to align with upcoming EU AI Act requirements for "human oversight" competency.

**Q5. When will the courses be available?**
A5. This is currently a **Stage VIII Strategic Definition**. The content is in blueprint phase. Pilot modules for Path 1 are scheduled for development in **H1 2026**.
